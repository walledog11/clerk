## General Concept

This SaaS lets users respond to customer messages that come in through multiple channels, such as TikTok, Instagram, business email, and Shopify Inbox. It will consolidate all those messages onto one dashboard that the user can use to see all their new support tickets and respond to them directly from the app. 

## App Functions

Collects customer messages from Instagram, TikTok, Shopify Chat, and business email
Pushes all the messages into an AI model that summarizes them with vital information such as order information, tracking number, etc.
User can then respond to the customer message by telling the AI agent (Ex: inform the customer with order #27492 that the changes they requested have been completed)
AI agent then formats a message and responds to customer through the channel they first reached out on

## How It Works

1. User connects Shopify Inbox, Instagram, business email, TikTok accounts.
2. User configures their agent by setting up notification intervals, automating certain types of messages, and attributes such as tone of the agent.
3. Customer messages from the connected accounts push into the agent's inbox and it begins to notify the user.

## Who Is This Built For?

This app is built for anyone who runs an e-commerce or online business. For solo business owners, it manages the bulk of their customer service demands and lets them dedicate more time to running business operations. For teams, it reduces their average amount of time to answer a customer service ticket. The agent can screen requests, automate them, or assign them to a team member if further assistance is needed. 
 
## Pricing Tiers

The free option will give users limited usage of the app and limit the amount of inbound support tickets it can get from the integrated platforms. 

The paid option gives small to medium size business full access to the app with no limitation. It will be priced at $12 a month 

The enterprise option is specifically designed for large businesses and the price will be available upon request.
